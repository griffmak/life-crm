# Life CRM — Categories

Configurable. Add via iMessage: "add category: [name]" | Remove: "remove category: [name]"

| Category | Urgency Default | Notes |
|----------|----------------|-------|
| Housing | high | Rent, lease renewal, renters insurance, utilities split |
| Bills & Subscriptions | medium | Recurring charges, price changes, cancellation candidates |
| Health | medium | Appointments, prescription refills, insurance, open enrollment |
| Social & Plans | low | Upcoming plans, trips, events, RSVPs, "said I'd do X with Y" |
| Taxes & Business | high | Quarterly estimates, domain renewals, GGA registration |
| NYC & Bureaucratic | medium | MetroCard, passport, REAL ID, voter registration |
| Digital | low | Annual software, password manager, domain renewals |
| Roommate Finance | high | Shared bills, who owes who, Splitwise |

## Urgency Override Rules
- Overdue rent → always HIGH regardless of category default
- Lease renewal within 90 days → HIGH (NYC law: landlord must give 60-day notice)
- Passport expiry within 6 months → HIGH (many countries require 6-month validity)
- Quarterly tax due within 14 days → HIGH
